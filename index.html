12 apps in 12 months

What is it?

This is an attempt to create 12 apps in 12 months, starting January 1st, 2017. It's about learning new stuff, validating ideas, avoiding over engineering and shipping apps.

The project was hugely inspired by Pieter Levels (@levelsio).

Who are you?

We are Rinat (@rinatkhanov) and Salavat (@khanov), two brothers, app makers from Russia.

Follow us on Twitter, it's going to be fun.
